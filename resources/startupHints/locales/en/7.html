Screenshot

A simple and very useful feature

Screenshot allows you to create a instant the state of the board (or your screen) during your course, to archive, share or reuse content.

In Board Mode

In the Pen palette, click , then select the area to capture. You can then choose:

  • Add to current page
  • Add to new page
  • Add to library — the capture is saved in the folder Pictures for later reuse.

The pen palette, page preview and library tab are not captured by lasso: only your annotations and your content are.

In Desktop Mode

Switch to Desktop Mode via to capture other software.

Two options are available:

  • Full screen capture
  • Capturing an area

As in Board Mode, choose where to add the capture.
