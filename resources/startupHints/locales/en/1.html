Welcome to OpenBoard

Here are some tips and tricks to help you get familiar with OpenBoard.

OpenBoard is an interactive whiteboard designed by teachers, for teachers. It offers four complementary modes for classroom use.

The 4 modes of OpenBoard

  • Board Mode

    The heart of OpenBoard: write, draw, annotate and manipulate objects on an infinite canvas.

  • Desktop Mode

    Display your computer desktop while keeping annotation tools close at hand.

  • Documents Mode

    Organize your pages, import media and prepare your educational sequences.

  • Web Mode

    Browse the Internet directly in OpenBoard and capture useful content.

Let's start with the first, and most important: Board Mode.
