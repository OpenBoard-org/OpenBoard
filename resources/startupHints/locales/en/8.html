Pictures

Access and organize your images

In OpenBoard you can access and add images in different ways from your library. The latter is located on the right of the screen. This is also where you can store sounds, videos and find different applications.

Organize your Pictures folder

Screenshots added to the library are grouped in the folder . You can create subfolders via (at the bottom of the library), then drag and drop your images into the different folders created.

Regardless of your position in the library, added images are automatically placed in the folder Pictures , which keeps your library organized.

Add images from the web

You can also search for royalty free images via integrated search engines, located in the folder Web Search .

No need to drag and drop: click on a found image to view its details and directly use the option Add to library. This is very practical when preparing lessons.
